How to make hearty 3-layer-lasagna

This is a special recipe a very close friend taught me how to make and best enjoyed hot out of the oven or chilled overnight in the fridge. Imagine a decedent creamy mac and cheese and a spicey spaghetti bolognese had a love child in your mouth.

The secret to this amazing dish is all in the cheese sauce, which needs to be consistently stirred over a low heat until it starts to become thicker, before adding the cheese and spices.

Ingredients

  • 250g Macaroni
  • 400ml Milk
  • 200g Cheddar Cheese
  • 300g Ground Beef
  • 1/2 Onion
  • Tomato Paste
  • 1 Can Tomato Puree
  • Spices
  • Chopped Herbs

Step by step Method

  1. Cook Macaroni until al dente
  2. Combine butter and corn starch in a sauce pan over low heat
  3. Slowly add milk and constantly stir until combination is disolved
  4. Keep stirring until the sauce thickens, don't stop stirring until the sauce is done!
  5. Add cheese, spices and herbs to your taste
  6. In a separate pan, add spices and butter and fry over low heat to bring out the flavour
  7. Add chopped onion and saute until soft
  8. Add ground beef and turn up the heat
  9. After the beef is cooked, add tomato puree and tomato paste and simmer for 20min
  10. Add a layer of lasagna sauce to the bottom of your favorite serving dish
  11. Add a layer of your mac and cheese over that.
  12. Repeat this step twice
  13. Sprinkle cheese on top and bake in the oven until golden

Onigiri

How to make yummy Onigiri Yaki

This is a dish a very close friend taught me how to make as I am an absolute anime freak and we both appreciate japanese culture and cuisine. This dish is very versatile as you can make almost any filling to add to the centre of your onigiri creation.

The main component to this dish is the sushi rice. Not just any rice will work, it has to be sushi rice for it to work. The sticky consistency makes it easier to mold a nd shape to rice around your filling of choice.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Chicken
  • Spice

Step by step Method

  1. Cook your sushi rice until soft, then set aside to cool and set
  2. Simmer spices in butter over low heat to bring out the flavour
  3. Add raw diced chicken cubes and fry until soft and tender
  4. Set aside to cool
  5. In your hand, shape the rice into the shape of a small bowl and scoop your chicken filling in the center
  6. Fold the sides of the rice over and add more rice on top to cover
  7. Wet you hands slightly before using the sides of both hands to shape the ball into a triangular shape/li>
  8. In a pre heated pan, fry and flip until both sides are golden

Flapperjack

How to make Flapperjacks

This is a tasty breakfast treat, but do not be fooled by its perceived simplicity as the fluffy texture comes with practice

The secret to making perfectly fluffy flapjacks every time is not over mixing and patience when cooking an low heat. I have learnt to wait for the bubbles to pop before flipping to ensure it become perfectly golden on both sides while preventing a raw centre.

Ingredients

  • 300g Self Raising Flour
  • 2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 Egg
  • 200ml Milk
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Oil

Step by step Method

  1. Combine dry ingredients
  2. Combine wet ingredients separately
  3. Slowly add wet to dry ingredients and mix until smooth
  4. Add oil to pan and scoop mixture into pan while swirling to spread evenly
  5. Wait for bubbles to pop and set before flipping
  6. Once edges are firm, the flapjack is done and can be transferred to a dishing plate
  7. Add a cube of butter to the top hot flapjack and drizzle your choice of pure honey over and enjoy
